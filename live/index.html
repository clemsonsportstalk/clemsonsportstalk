Clemson Sports Talk Chatroom

We want the chat to be a pleasant and safe place for all chatters. The primary focus of this chat is content related to the live shows. Keeping that in mind, these are the rules:

  1. Keep chat conversation at a family friendly level, as there are a wide range of ages in our chat. Self-censorship of swearing is not considered "family friendly".
  2. No offensive references to a person or group by race, religion, gender, or orientation.
  3. PLEASE DON'T TYPE IN ALL CAPS.
  4. Do not harass other chatters. You can respectfully disagree.
  5. Do not repeat yourself over and over to try to get noticed, as this is disruptive to the chat.

The most important (and simplest) rule: Use common sense, and don't be a jerk.
